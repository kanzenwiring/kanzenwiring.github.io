About

After 25 Years of Industrial experience and 10 years in Automotive wiring harness industry, this company has been started by Managing Partners in Gurgaon, the Automotive Hub of India.

We are willing to serve various Automotive customers who manufacture 2 wheelers and 4 wheelers parts to OE’s, initially, and in 2nd phase to OE directly with vision of providing best quality, productivity, and cost competitiveness

Major goal of the partners is to give 100% customer satisfaction by supplying quality product to the Indian Automotive Industry and to their Tier1 supplier.
